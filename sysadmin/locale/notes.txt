------------------------------
generate locales:
	1. uncomment desired locales from /etc/locale.gen
	2. locale-gen

------------------------------
list generated locales:
	localedef --list-archive
	localectl list-locales

------------------------------
set the locale
	meth1:
		localectl set-locale LANG=locale_to_set_to
	meth2:
		edit /etc/locale.conf:
		LANG=locale

NOTE:
	locale is not enough to display different languages, must also have
	supporting font
	?recommend noto-* fonts?
